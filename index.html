Xiaohao Xu

I am currently a researcher at the Foundamental Vision Group of SenseTime Research, where I research on generic multimodal perception.

Before that, I achieved my Bachelor's degree at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2022, advised by Prof. Weiming Shen and Prof. Bin Han. I took a wonderful internship at Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) from 2020 to 2022.

I am interested in robotics, computer vision, machine learning, and signal processing.
