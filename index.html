Xiaohao Xu

Currently, I am a researcher at the Fundamental Vision Group of SenseTime Research headed by Prof. Xiaogang Wang. My current research focus is on generic multimodal perception of humans, automobiles, and complex environments.

Prior to that, I achieved my Bachelor's degree from the Elite Program of Mechanical Engineering at Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2022, advised by Prof. Weiming Shen and Prof. Bin Han. I took several wonderful research internships at Microsoft Research Asia (MSRA) mentored by Dr. Jinglu Wang, Sinovation Ventures AI Institute advised by Prof. Yan Song and Prof. Ming Zhou, and Wuhan National Laboratory for Optoelectronics mentored by Prof. Ke Zhou and Prof. Yu Liu.

I believe in the future of human-machine symbiosis. My past research projects focus on (1) human-in-the-loop designs of wearable devices, robots, and intelligent systems; (2) robust/reliable algorithms to perceive humans or generic objects of the environment.

I am interested in Robotics, Human-Robot Systems/Collaboration, (Multimodal)Perception and Manipulation, Video Tracking and Segmentation, and Learning-based Algorithms and Models.

Selected Projects

Human-AI Co-writing System.
Collaborated with my friends: Z. Li, X. Tang, Z. Fei, and H. Zheng.
- Design and build a Human-Al Co-witing System based on the Chinese large-scale pre-trained language model of the large-scale science fiction corpus.
- Propose a new paradigm of human-computer collaborative writing is proposed. After users input the first sentence, the machine outputs top candidates of the next sentence. Then the finished sentences are further filtered and modified by users. Furthermore, the whole article is generated by continuous autoregression.
- The system was reported and praised by some mainstream media like The People's Daily, South China Morning Post, and Yahoo News, etc.

Design and source code from Leonid Keselman and Jon Barron
